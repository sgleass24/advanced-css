How to Make Banana Bread

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of coarse salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup flour

Step 2: Mix Ingredients

In a mixing bowl start by mashing the bananas, then add the melted butter, baking soda, salt, sugar egg and vanilla. Once smooth add in the flour, mix thoroughly.

Step 3: Bake

Add batter to a 4x8in loaf pan, bake at 350degrees for 50min. Let cool 5-10min before serving.
